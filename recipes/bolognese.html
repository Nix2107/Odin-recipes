Ragu Bolognese Recipe

Description:

Bolognese spaghetti or simply Bolognese is an italian dish made from spaghetti with the meat-based bolognese sauce which gives its name to the dish.

Ingredients:

  • a half-cup of extra virgin olive oil
  • a half-cup of butter
  • one cup of minced onion
  • a half-cup of minced celery
  • a quarter-cup of minced carrot
  • one pound of ground veal
  • one pound ground pork
  • a half-pound of ground beef
  • a quarter-cup of finely chopped pancetta bacon
  • a half-cup of milk
  • two cups of tomato sauce
  • two cups of beef broth

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil and butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Saute onion, celery and carrots until soft.
  3. Add veal, pork, beef and pancetta, and cook until evenly brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
  4. Pour in milk, and cook until liquid has evaporated, about 15 minutes.
  5. tir in tomato sauce and beef broth.
  6. Cover, and simmer 2 1/2 hours.
