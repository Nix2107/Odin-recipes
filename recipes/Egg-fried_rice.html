Egg-fried Rice Recipe

Description:

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients
such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients:

  • 250g long grain rice
  • three tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • one finely chopped onion
  • four eggs
  • two spring onions, sliced, to serve

Steps:

  1. Cook the rice following pack instructions, then drain, spread it out to steam-dry and set aside.
  2. Heat two tablespoons of the oil in a large wok over a high heat.
  3. Add the onion and fry until lightly browned, around 5 mins.
  4. Add the rice, stir and toast for about 3 mins, then move to the side of the pan.
  5. Add the remaining oil, then tip in the egg mixture.
  6. Leave to cook a little, then mix in with the rice. Stir vigorously to coat the grains.
  7. Tip into a serving bowl and scatter over the spring onion to serve.
